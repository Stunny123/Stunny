Advantages

Being able to cook with solar energy without using electricity and gas. We achieve this by using adjustable mirrors to reflect the sunlight and heat your food.

Read More

Sunlight is reflected with mirrors positioned at a certain angle so the sunlight can touch the pan and heat it by causing the particles to vibrate. Mirrors reflect the light because they are electrically conductive (light is an electromagnetic field). The law of reflection states light is reflected at the same angle as it touches the surface.

Our team is made up of people that want to find alternatives to electric cooking, high gas prices and help people that do not have access to good natural gas infrastructure.

There are a lot of people using normal energy and wasting gas, which can lead to contamination and serious problems in Earth, so we made Stunny to stop those problems.

How to use Stunny?

Once you open the portable package in a sunny area, you will place your food on top of the metal base placed at the center of the circle. You have to adjust the mirrors in a way that the sunlight heats your food.
